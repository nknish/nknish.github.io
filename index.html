Hi, I'm Noah! I'm a sophomore at Pitt, but I'm originally from the DMV. I'm an aspiring software engineer majoring in CS, minoring in philosophy, and seeking a certificate in sustainability. I'm also studying Spanish when I get the chance. I love playing ultimate, listening to music (especially live!), playing guitar and bass, hiking, reading fiction and nonfiction books, going to the gym, attending synagogue, and working on coding side projects. I hope you enjoy my website :)
